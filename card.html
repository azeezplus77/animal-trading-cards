Brown Bears

In fall a brown bear may eat 40 kilograms of food each day, and it may weigh twice as much before the hibernation.

  • Scientfic name: Ursus arctos
  • Life span: 30 years
  • Length:1.4-2.8m
  • Habitat:Forests and mountains

Brown bears tend to be solitary animals, except for females and their cubs, Most brown bears are not highly carnivorous, as they derive up to 90% of their dietary food energy from vegetable matter, they eat roots, insects, mammals, fish, reptiles, and of course, honey. source
